## Professional transport as a strategic choice

Choosing a carrier is not a purely logistical decision. It is a trade-off that directly impacts margins, customer satisfaction and the ability to honor contractual commitments.

In France, nearly 90% of domestic freight transport is carried out by road. But depending on volumes, distances and the nature of goods, other modes (rail, maritime, air, multimodal) may prove more relevant.

## Transport modes

### Road transport

Flexible, fast and suited to short and medium distances. Ideal for last-mile delivery and express courier.

### Maritime transport

Essential for large international flows. The container remains the reference unit. Long lead times but very competitive unit cost on large volumes.

### Rail transport

Relevant for long distances and heavy goods. Cheaper than road beyond 500 km but constrained by available network.

### Air transport

Reserved for high-value, perishable or urgent products. High cost but unbeatable lead times.
