## Why used remains a smart choice

The used car market in France represents over 5 million transactions per year, three times more than new cars. A lower purchase price, depreciation already absorbed by the first owner and a very broad offer across all segments explain this momentum.

With rising prices in the new car market, recent used vehicles (under 3 years old) have become the most rational alternative for most budgets.

## Setting the total budget

The purchase price is only part of the cost. Anticipate:

- Registration fees, variable by region and vehicle
- Insurance, ranging from 700 to 1,500 euros per year
- Routine maintenance and possible repairs
- Fuel or energy depending on the powertrain

## Key checks before buying

A technical inspection less than 6 months old is mandatory for vehicles over 4 years. Request the complete service booklet, repair invoices and the official Histovec certificate showing the vehicle's history. A road test of at least 20 minutes, in town and on the motorway, helps detect abnormal noise, vibrations or engine and gearbox weaknesses.
