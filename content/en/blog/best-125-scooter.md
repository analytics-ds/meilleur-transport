## How to choose your 125 scooter {#choosing}

### Main use: urban or mixed

- **100% urban (under 15 km per trip)**: PCX 125, Django 125, Agility 125
- **Mixed urban + beltway (15-40 km per trip)**: XMAX 125, Medley 125, Forza 125
- **Long daily trips (>40 km)**: BMW C 400 X or faired 125 bike

### Boot size

- **1 helmet** enough: PCX, Django, Agility (18-30 L)
- **2 helmets** needed: XMAX, Forza, Medley (36-48 L)

### Thermal or electric

- **Thermal** if range > 100 km/day or no home charging
- **Electric** if exclusive urban use, easy home charging

### Budget

- **Under 3 000 euros**: Agility 125, Sym Fiddle
- **3 500 to 5 000 euros**: PCX 125, Django 125
- **5 000 to 7 000 euros**: XMAX 125, Medley 125, Silence S01
- **Over 7 000 euros**: Forza, BMW C 400 X

## Practical tips for first purchase {#tips}

### Mandatory 7h training

Since 2011, B licence holders must complete a **7-hour training** to ride a 125 cc scooter. **Cost: 250 to 400 euros** at an accredited school.

### Mandatory equipment

**Homologated helmet** (ECE 22.06) and **homologated CE gloves** are mandatory. 68 euro fine otherwise.

### Mandatory insurance

**Third-party insurance minimum** is mandatory. Count:
- **Third-party**: 300 to 500 €/year
- **Third-party + theft + fire**: 500 to 700 €/year
- **Full coverage**: 600 to 900 €/year

### Regular maintenance

A 125 scooter needs **servicing every 6 000 km**. **Average cost: 150 to 250 euros per service**.

## Frequently asked questions {#faq}

<details>
<summary>What's the best 125 scooter in 2026?</summary>

The **Honda PCX 125** at 3 649 euros remains the best value: record consumption (2.1 l/100 km), recognised reliability, easy resale. For superior comfort and long rides, the **Yamaha XMAX 125** at 5 999 euros dominates the grand touring category.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I ride a 125 scooter with B licence?</summary>

Yes, provided you've had the **B licence for over 2 years** and completed the **mandatory 7-hour training**. Without these 2 conditions, you need the **A1 licence** via practical exam.
</details>

<details>
<summary>What consumption for a 125 scooter?</summary>

Between **2.1 l/100 km for a Honda PCX 125** (best on market) and **3.2 l/100 km for a sporty scooter like the Piaggio Medley 125**. Average: **2.5 to 3 l/100 km** in normal urban riding.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Thermal or electric 125 scooter?</summary>

**Thermal** if your use includes trips over 50 km non-stop or if you have no home charging. **Electric** if exclusive urban use with home charging.
</details>

<details>
<summary>How much insurance for a 125 scooter?</summary>

Between **400 and 900 euros per year** depending on formula (third-party or full), geographic zone, driver profile and model.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Which 125 scooter for 2 people?</summary>

Favour **GT scooters with large seat**: **Yamaha XMAX 125** or **Honda Forza 125**.
</details>

<details>
<summary>How long does a 125 scooter last?</summary>

With **regular maintenance** (servicing every 6 000 km), a brand 125 scooter (Honda, Yamaha, Piaggio) can reach **80 000 to 100 000 km** without major intervention.
</details>
